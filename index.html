🧪 Acid-Base Sorter

A quick-thinking science sorting game!
Test your knowledge of acids, bases, and neutral substances.

How to Play

  • 🧫 Substances fall from the top of the screen, each with a name and pH-related clue.
  • 🗑️ Three bins are at the bottom: Acid, Neutral, and Base.
  • ⬅️➡️ Switch the active bin using ← → keys, A/D, or by tapping a bin directly.
  • ✅ Catch each substance in its correct bin to score points.
  • ❌ Wrong bin = lose a life (❤️). Letting it fall also costs a small penalty.
  • ⏱️ One continuous timer — sort as many as you can before time runs out!
  • ⭐ Earn up to 3 stars based on your final score.
